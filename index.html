Book your holiday adventure with Comfy House and feel like home!


If you are looking for an exotic holiday destination and you are bored with hotels full of people, you have come to the right place. I offer my lovely house in Las Terrenas, Dominican Republic for rent. My house has 4 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms, a large kitchen and a living room. If you don't have anyone to leave your pet with, I'd love to meet him as well. In addition, you can enjoy the beautiful garden and swimming pool. It is only a moment to the beach.

What else?


There are things which make your holiday more pleasent and unforgettable:

  • there are 4 bicycles at your disposal
  • you can use sunbeds
  • nearest supermarket 5 minutes from the house
  • there are a lot of bars with music and restaurants with amazing cuisine
  • in the neighborhood you can find water equipment, car or quad rentals
  • there are also tourist points where you can book excursions and explore the island better

Price


The minimum number of nights is 3. For one double room, the price is 70 euros per night. There is no charge for children up to 6 years of age. There is also no extra charge for pets. If you are interested in renting a room or the whole house, please go to the booking tab and ask for availability.
